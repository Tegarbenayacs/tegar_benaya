Intro

What I am all about.

In the harmony between thunderous drum rhythms and the complex codes he crafts, Tegar Benaya presents the unique story of a software engineering student pursuing two passions simultaneously. Each time he sits behind his drum set, the world seems to fade away, leaving only a series of passionately executed beats. However, when he transitions from the darkness of the musical stage, Tegar Benaya immerses himself in the abstract realm of the digital world, designing algorithms and coding programs with the same precision as his dramatic performance on the drums.

With each drumbeat and line of code he creates, Tegar Benaya demonstrates that creativity can be found at the intersection of art and technology. He is a symbol of diverse talents, inspiring others to embrace their various passions and explore the vast world where drum melodies and algorithms harmoniously resonate, forming a unique and captivating narrative of life.
